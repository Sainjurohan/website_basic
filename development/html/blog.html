Impact of Technology

Introduction to Technology

Technology can be defined as the applications, systems, or devices that are the result of scientific knowledge and problem solving used by us humans to solve real-world problems. Technology has become part of our daily life making us rely on them to solve problems efficiently and swiftly. When we think of technology the first thing that comes to our mind is the Internet. Simply, the internet we use is a product of technology that enables us to browse the data and information from around the world. The development of technology takes a lot of effort and skilled manpower with technical and logical knowledge but the use of technologies thus designed is simple.To keep it simple, let’s take smartphones as an example. In this modern era, the smartphone has become an inseparable part of human life which is simple to use and is widely used by people around the globe but we know the invention of the smartphone has evolution history from the simple radio to pagers, pagers to the simple phone, and finally to smartphones after many decades of research hardwork and research.

“Technology is the use of scientific knowledge for practical purposes or application”.. Thus, whenever we use scientific knowledge, we’re using technology. In addition, technology usually involves specific equipment designed for problem-solving. These equipment are dazzlingly complex or simple in nature. Equipment can be anything from a simple resistor or chips to complex IC (Integrated Circuits). From simple machines to AI incorporated complex systems we are surrounded by technology.

Technology has connected the whole world verifying the sentence, “The world is a global village”. The computers, smartphones, and communication networks we use are just some examples of technology. We are surrounded by technology. Without technology our daily life also might get hindered as we individuals rely on technology to interact with peers and friends through social networks, carrying out everyday activities such as banking and shopping, or simply surfing the web for information and knowledge.

There are different types of technologies depending upon the area of use. Such as Mechanical Technology, Electronics Technology, Information Technology, Industrial Technology, Medical Technology, communication technology, etc. Perhaps the type of technology that we are most familiar with maybe Electronics, communication technology, and Information technology as we make the most use of them in our day-to-day life.

Technology in Communication

The advancement of technology over the decades has made communication faster and convenient. Communication tools signify how quickly technology has evolved. In past, writing a letter, sending fax, pagers, wired telephones were the best way to communicate. But today, we have a more efficient and convenient way at our fingertips. We can use social media, use SMS, video chat, email, or make a call to communicate.

Technology in Banking

The conventional way of visiting banks for withdrawal and transfer of money is now outdated with the introduction of platforms like banking applications, PayPal, etc. These platforms allow us to make transactions like sending and receiving money from any location making use of the internet. Paying bills by scheduling them is also facilitated. We also don’t need to carry cash during our travels as we can use a banking app as an e-wallet and make payments anywhere anytime.

Technology in TV

With the advancement of mobile devices we can watch, or “Stream” the TV shows we like with ease. In addition, the introduction of streaming services like Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Hulu, etc. all offer us various shows and series from across the world. Unlike traditional cable tv, we don’t have to pay for the channels we don’t use. Also, the smart TV these days makes use of the internet providing access to more content according to the genre we like. In addition, new TVs are able to display picture quality far beyond that in the past providing us with a realistic experience.

Technology in Dating

The advancement of social media platforms with the advancement of technology made us ease of communicating with the ones we know and also meeting new people. Also, with the introduction of online dating sites, we can register on a website, find a match, and start to communicate at our own pace. These dating sites prioritize protecting our privacy while getting to know someone.

These dating platforms have millions of registered users and received good reviews. These platforms help people connect with their matches using its complex algorithm. It is comfortable and straightforward. It is helpful to get to know people and also keep up with our busy schedules.

Technology in IoT

Almost all the technology that we use today is made possible due to the internet. The data which are used as input in a simple app to a complex AI system is synced in presence of the internet. The IoT uses internet technology which is not always accessible on screens. IoT syncs then real-world objects using the internet making them remotely accessible.

Almost every real-world object uses IoT which helps in smooth data flow and allows its transfer and access smooth. Some of the examples of the objects that use IoT are fitness trackers, doorbells, security cameras, health devices like ECG, blood pressure monitors, MRI, etc., smart TV, etc. These devices make use of the internet to sync and transfer data.
